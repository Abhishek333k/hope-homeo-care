Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

At Hope Homeo Care, your privacy is paramount. We explicitly limit our data collection to the information necessary to facilitate your care. Through our booking portal, we collect your full name, phone/WhatsApp number, preferred appointment date, and brief symptoms exclusively for the purpose of secure consultation booking.

2. Data Security

Your health data is not kept in unverified digital registries. We utilize a highly secure Firebase Firestore backend infrastructure. All information submitted through our portal is fully encrypted in transit ensuring your communication remains private and protected before reaching our internal systems.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We believe in absolute confidentiality. Patient data is never sold, traded, or shared with third-party marketing, analytics, or advertising agencies. Your symptoms and identity remain strictly between you and Dr. Joshua’s clinical team.
