Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Hope Homeo Care (operated by Dr. K. Nikhil Joshua), we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Telemedicine Practice Guidelines of India.

1. Information We Collect

To provide effective homeopathic care and maintain our digital Patient Portal, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your full name, mobile number, age, gender, and blood group.
  • Medical Data: Descriptions of your symptoms, medical history, past prescriptions, and consultation logs.
  • Technical Data: Your phone number is used exclusively for OTP-based secure authentication to access your private Patient Portal.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly for medical and operational purposes:

  • To evaluate symptoms, perform triage, and provide homeopathic diagnoses and prescriptions.
  • To schedule, manage, and send notifications regarding your clinic appointments.
  • To securely authenticate your identity via SMS OTP when accessing the Patient Portal.
  • To maintain accurate Electronic Medical Records (EMR) as required by Indian healthcare regulations.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase). We employ strict security rules ensuring that your digital medical records can only be accessed by your verified phone number and the clinic's authorized medical staff. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or medical data with third-party marketers or advertisers.

4. Telemedicine & Digital Prescriptions

Consultations conducted via online triage or our digital booking system comply with Indian Telemedicine Guidelines. Prescriptions generated and stored within your Patient Portal are valid digital records. By utilizing our online services, you consent to the digital transmission and storage of your health information.

5. Your Patient Rights

You have the right to request a review of your medical records. For clinical safety and integrity, core identifiers (like your registered mobile number) cannot be changed directly through the online portal. To request rectifications or data deletion, please contact the clinic directly in person or via our official communication channels.
